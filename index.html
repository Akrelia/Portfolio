About me

Since I was young, I've been passionate about programming, especially video games.

I started with some scripts on mIRC before clumsily trying my hand at C++.

After a little pause I delved into web development, which was my first love (and something I've recently rediscovered with modern JavaScript). Then came software development, which allowed me to solidify my skills and reach a higher level in my developer journey.And finally video games. I thought transitioning from software to games would be easy, but it's a whole different world, especially game design, so the transition was tough. But once I overcame the challenges, it was pure joy.

After graduating from university, I mosttly worked as a freelance developer and had the opportunity to spend a lot of time working on personal projects.

By exploring all possible domains, I'm happy to be versatile, which allows me to be truly well-rounded in my everyday work.
